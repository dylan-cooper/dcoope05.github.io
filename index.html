Introduction

My first co-op position came close to home, right here at the University of Guelph. I worked in the Enterprise Applications department with a new system that was still being explored during my time with the team. I worked heavily in C# using the .NET MVC framework as well as Knockout.JS which we used on the front-end. This was my first experience with real-world software development and I experienced a lot:

  • Requirements gathering with our clients
  • Learning and teaching a new language and framework or two
  • Exploring new technologies and sharing them with peers
  • Writing two lines of documentation for every line of code
